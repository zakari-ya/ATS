# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Job seekers and early-career to mid-career professionals who want to review a
CV against a specific job description before applying. They usually arrive with
an existing CV PDF and a copied job post, and they need quick, private, and
clear feedback about what their CV shows well and what is still missing.

## Product Purpose
CVMatch is an ATS-style CV checker SaaS. It lets an authenticated user upload a
text-based CV PDF, paste a job description, and receive structured match
feedback with a backend-controlled score, missing requirements, strong points,
and practical improvement suggestions. Success means the user understands what
to improve before applying, without the app pretending to make hiring
decisions.

## Brand Personality
Premium, calm, trustworthy. The product should feel serious, private, and clear
rather than noisy, gimmicky, or AI-hyped.

## Anti-references
- Generic blue or purple AI startup landing pages
- Hiring-decision language such as accepted, rejected, pass, or fail
- Loud dashboards with cramped cards and crowded metrics
- Fake testimonials, fake employer logos, or exaggerated guarantees
- Playful or casual visual language that weakens trust

## Design Principles
- Explain the match clearly, not dramatically.
- Keep privacy and user control visible throughout the flow.
- Let backend validation and scoring lead the trust model.
- Make every major action easy to understand on mobile first.
- Prefer calm, premium clarity over decorative complexity.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast and semantic structure across marketing and app
surfaces. Keep forms labeled, focus states visible, tap targets comfortable on
mobile, and avoid relying on color alone for meaning. Respect reduced motion
preferences and keep loading, error, and empty states readable for non-technical
users.
